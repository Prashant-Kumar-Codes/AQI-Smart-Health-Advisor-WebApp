Health Impact

▼

Who is at Risk?

  • 👶 Children: Developing lungs, breathe more air per body weight
  • 👴 Elderly: Susceptible to particle pollution effects
  • 🫁 Respiratory Conditions: Asthma, COPD patients highly sensitive
  • ❤️ Heart Conditions: Risk of heart attacks and arrhythmias
  • 🏃 Active Outdoors: Athletes breathe more polluted air

Short-term Effects:

Eye, nose, throat irritation, coughing, chest tightness, shortness of breath, reduced lung function.

Long-term Effects:

Chronic exposure can lead to reduced lung function, respiratory diseases, cardiovascular problems, and increased cancer risk.

🌱

Take Action

▼

Individual Actions:

  • 🚌 Use public transportation, carpool, or bike
  • 💡 Conserve energy at home
  • 🌡️ Use energy-efficient settings
  • 🚗 Maintain vehicle properly
  • 🔥 Avoid burning wood or trash
  • 🌳 Plant trees and support green spaces
  • ♻️ Reduce, reuse, and recycle

Protect Yourself:

  • 📱 Monitor air quality regularly
  • 😷 Keep N95/KN95 masks available
  • 💨 Use HEPA air purifiers at home
  • 🏃 Plan outdoor activities wisely
  • 🪟 Keep windows closed on bad days
  • 💊 Keep medications updated

Community Action:

Support clean air initiatives, advocate for stricter emissions, participate in tree-planting, and encourage workplace air quality measures.
